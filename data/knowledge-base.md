## Common Questions

### When will my order ship?
Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a tracking email once shipped.

### Can I change my shipping address?
Yes, we can update your address if the order has not yet shipped. Contact us as soon as possible.

### My tracking number isn't updating.
Tracking can take 24-48 hours to activate after the shipping label is created. If it has been more than 5 days with no updates, our fulfillment team will investigate.

### Are your supplements safe to take with medication?
We always recommend consulting your healthcare provider before starting any new supplement, especially if you are taking medication.

### How do I cancel my order?
Order cancellations need to be handled by our team. Claude will escalate cancellation requests.
